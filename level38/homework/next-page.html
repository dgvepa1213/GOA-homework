Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

A Genius of Classical Music

Early Life

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was born on January 27, 1756, in Salzburg, Austria. He was a child prodigy, composing music by the age of five and performing before European royalty.

Career Highlights

  • Composed over 600 works, including symphonies, operas, chamber music, and piano concertos.
  • Notable operas include *The Magic Flute*, *Don Giovanni*, and *The Marriage of Figaro*.
  • Influenced by and collaborated with composers like Haydn and Beethoven.

Personal Life

Mozart married Constanze Weber in 1782. Despite his extraordinary talent, he faced financial difficulties throughout his life and passed away at the young age of 35, on December 5, 1791.

Legacy

Mozart’s music remains timeless, celebrated for its technical mastery and emotional depth. His work continues to inspire musicians and listeners around the world.
